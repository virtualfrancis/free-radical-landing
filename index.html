The go/no-go call, before you leave

Every report opens with a plain verdict for your activity. Green means go. Yellow means think twice. Red means stay home. The details are there if you want them.

GO

Use caution for skiing. Avalanche danger is Considerable (3/5). Travel cautiously and avoid slopes above 35 degrees.

HAZARD
