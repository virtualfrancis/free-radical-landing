Conditions built for outdoor objectives

All the data sources you'd check anyway, pulled together by activity and shown in the order that actually matters.

🎯
Activity Modes
Switch between skiing, climbing, hiking, diving, and fishing. Each mode shows only the conditions relevant to that objective.
🌡️
Weather
Current temperature, wind speed and direction, humidity, freezing level, UV index, and barometric pressure trend.
📅
Forecast
Multi-day outlook so you can plan ahead and pick the right window for your objective.
❄️
Snow & Avalanche
SNOTEL snow depth and water equivalent paired with current avalanche danger level from the regional avalanche center.
🌊
Tides & Moon
Tide times and heights for coastal objectives, plus moon phase and illumination percentage for night travel planning.
🎒
Trips & Gear
Plan trips, build gear loadouts, and track what you packed. Rate your outings and keep notes on conditions you found.
